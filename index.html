Agricultural Boron Fertilizers

Boron is an essential micro-nutrient for plant growth and is classified as a micro-nutrient fertilizer. It promotes the transport and metabolism of sugars in crops, increases sucrose synthesis, and accelerates sucrose translocation, thereby increasing the seed setting rate and fruit set rate of crops. When boron is deficient, the seeds cannot develop normally or may not form at all, seriously affecting the yield and quality of crops.
